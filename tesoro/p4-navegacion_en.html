Towards the Baths

"Cross now the heart of the city to the place where steam and water comforted the body after exhaustion. My father and I raised the Baths of Barcino there for the pride of the colony.

Look for the square that hides behind the great palace of the city government. There where history plunges beneath the ground, I wait for you."
