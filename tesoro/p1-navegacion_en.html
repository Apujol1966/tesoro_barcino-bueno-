The First Step

Follow the path of my words...

"From the place where your journey begins, pass through the fountain of the most innocent, with their baskets in hand, and continue along the Angel’s portal as planned.

Near the end of the way, when you hesitate, leave the women carrying amphorae to your right and don't wait.

To Barcino, my city, the blood of life must flow, through veins of stone, as you will soon know.

There, where its great artery begins and ends, where seven figures rest, by the Sun and Moon’s defense, engraved among them, my final voice transcends."

Navigation Clue:

Stand at Plaça Catalunya. Walk down the Portal de l'Àngel avenue to the end.

You will reach the Cathedral square (Plaça Nova). Look for the remains of the Roman Aqueduct attached to the city wall.
