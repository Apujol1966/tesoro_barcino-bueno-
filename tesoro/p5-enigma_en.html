The Eternal Dwelling

You have reached the edge of time.

"You have reached the edge of the ancient city, where the noise of your time stops before the silence of my ancestors.

A great rusted metal wall now guards the entrance to our eternal home. On it, the men of your century have engraved the acronym of those who today guard my history and the secrets of Barcino. If you are in front of the gate, you will see them shining in the metal."

That is not the correct acronym. Look closely at the letters on the metal plate.
Hint: Look for the largest letters on the rusted steel plate, right below "VIA SEPULCRAL ROMANA". It is the name of the Barcelona History Museum.
