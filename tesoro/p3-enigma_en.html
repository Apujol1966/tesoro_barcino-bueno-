The Mirror of the City

Exactly! It is the Archive of the Crown of Aragon.

🏛️ City Archives:

"Look closely at the foundations of this place... They have raised these walls using the imposing stones of our ancient defensive wall. Centuries will pass, but the unbreakable foundations of my beloved Barcino will always support the weight of history."

Your feet are in the right place.

"Well, I hope your cunning has brought you here before the black hands of Barcino. This trial will be difficult; seek help in the reflection of history.

Straight ahead, imposing iron you will find, and only if you look through its eye will it be you who you see. You must look from where something as ancient as me still remains. Now that you know where to look, the next clue you will find.

If you have reached the exact spot, the reflection of the city will speak to you. To open my (imaginary) chest, you will need to count from that spot that acts as a mirror of the square:

  • How many bells can you see?
  • How many gargoyles can you count on the building where the Aragon documents are kept?
  • How many angels are needed to hold the shield in a corner?
  • How many statues crown the cathedral?
  • How many fountains give water to the square?

If you are able to see it all, you will be able to read my message. Now that you have the code, I will give you three options so that you can check if you have looked correctly through the mirror of the city:

I) CODE I - XI - I - II - I
II) CODE II - XI - II - I - I
III) CODE II - X - II - I - I

Which of the three codes have you deciphered?"

That code will not open the chest. Go back to the glass window and count again.
Positioning hint: Look for the iron sculpture (Chillida). Look through its gaps towards the glass fence on the ground. Stand on that glass: from there you will see the 2 bells, the 11 gargoyles on the archive, and the other elements.
