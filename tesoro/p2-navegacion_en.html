Towards the Temple

EXACTLY! THE TEMPLE OF AUGUSTUS.

🏛️ City Archives:

The Temple of Augustus in Barcino was built in the 1st century for the imperial cult of Caesar Augustus, who gave permission to found the colony of Barcino.

The temple was erected over the Forum, in the current Gothic Quarter, at the highest point of Mount Táber.

Find it to follow the path that Lucio has left us!

Navigation Clue:

Look for Carrer Paradís, at the highest point of the Gothic Quarter, next to Plaça Sant Jaume. Inside a small courtyard, you will find the heart of the ancient city.
