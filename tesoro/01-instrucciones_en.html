Before You Begin

  • YOUR MISSION Solve historical riddles and follow Lucio's clues to discover the Treasure of Barcino.
  • RIDDLE HELP If you get stuck on a puzzle, don't worry! After 3 failed attempts, a help button will appear.
  • NAVIGATION HELP To get from one stop to another, we'll give you a clue. If that's not enough, by passing small attention tests, you can unlock a Direct Clue and a Map.
  • PLAY AT YOUR OWN PACE... OR COMPETE! Enjoy the walk without a hurry... or challenge your friends to see who deciphers the mystery first!
  • DIRECT HELP If at any time you have a technical problem or a question about the game, contact us by pressing the "I Need Help" button at the bottom of this page.
